VIKRAM CREATIONS PRIVATE LIMITED

Established in the year 1999, Vikram Creations Pvt. Ltd, is a well known fashion house based out of New Delhi, India. The comapany is a renowned as a manufacturer, wholesaler, trader and supplier of women fashion. The company owns and operates 7 brands namely Elomelo, Style Girl, A Sweet Design, Elly Ross, Style Plus, Denim Studio and Active Spirits, each with their own distinct identity. All these branded garments are especially designed and crafted by a well trianed team of in-house designers.
